Tower Cost Tool (Coordinate Tracking)

This tool tracks towers using their **X, Z coordinates** to accurately determine upgrade levels and replace costs.
**Supported cost formats:**

  • Place: `place(X, Y, Z, "TowerName", cost)`
  • Upgrade: `upgrade(X, Y, Z, cost)`
